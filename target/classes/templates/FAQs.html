About Smart Grid System :

The smart grid system is able to collect the consumed data and pass them among some meters untill they reach the head-end system.

With this site you can:

  • Show your consumption during a specific period, you chose.
  • Show your devices consumptions.
  • Pay your bill.
  • Contact us if you have any problem.

To view your consumption:

Firstly, you have to choose the first date and the end date, to view the consumpution during this period and then click the button.

Firstly you could see the price and the last payment date.

You have to enter some data about your credit card and then press the button Pay.
